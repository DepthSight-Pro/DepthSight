# Backtesting Basics

Backtesting is the process of testing a trading strategy on historical data. In DepthSight, we use a high-performance engine for the most accurate results.

## How to run a backtest:

1. Open the **Strategy Editor**.
2. Select or create a configuration.
3. Click the **Run Backtest** button.
4. Specify the time period and initial balance.

### Backtest Results:

After completion, you will see:
* **PNL**: Net profit or loss.
* **Win Rate**: Percentage of winning trades.
* **Max Drawdown**: Maximum drawdown.
* **Sharpe Ratio**: Strategy efficiency ratio.
